ERDDAP > Log In

This ERDDAP is configured to let you log in by signing in to your Google email account, including Google-managed accounts such as @noaa.gov accounts. This allows ERDDAP to verify your identity (name and email address) and access your profile image, but does not give ERDDAP access to your emails, your Google Drive, or any other private information. Anyone can log in but the administrator of this ERDDAP determines who gets access to additional datasets.

You are not logged in. (You don't have to be logged in to access ERDDAP's publicly available datasets.)

Problems?

If you have problems logging in:

  • If the Google button says you are "Signed in", but clicking "Log into ERDDAP" doesn't log you into ERDDAP, wait 10 seconds and click "Log into ERDDAP" again. If that doesn't work, wait another 10 seconds and try again.
     
  • Make sure your browser is set to allow cookies:
    • In Chrome, use "Settings : Show advanced settings : Privacy/Content Settings : Cookies/Allow local data to be set"
    • In Firefox, use "Options : Privacy : History/Accept cookies from sites"
    • In Internet Explorer, use "Tools : Internet Options : Privacy : Settings : Advanced : First Party Cookies/Accept"
    • In Opera, use "Menu : Settings : Privacy and Security : Cookies/Allow local data to be set"
    • In Safari, use "Safari : Preferences : Security : Cookies and website data : Allow"
    If you are making ERDDAP requests from a computer program (other than a browser), cookies are hard to work with. Sorry.
     
  • Contact the administrator of this ERDDAP: Lynn deWitt (email: lynn dot dewitt at noaa dot gov).
