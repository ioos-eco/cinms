Once thought to be common throughout Southern California, these critically endangered "gentle giants" can weigh up to 800lbs and live more than 80 years! A curious fish that specializes in eating crustaceans and almost any fish, giant black seabass are known to investigate SCUBA divers as they cruise through kelp forests
